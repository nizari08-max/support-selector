ASME B31.1 vs B31.3
B31.1 (Power Piping) — Table 121.5 gives direct span values for standard steel pipe (water service and steam/gas/air). Widely used for utility and power plant systems.

B31.3 (Process Piping) — Does not publish a span table directly. Values here are from Shell DEP engineering guidelines, commonly adopted for process plant design.
